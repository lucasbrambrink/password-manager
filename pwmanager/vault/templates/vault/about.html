SimpleVault

This platform was designed to simply & securely store login credentials. By better managing the multitude of passwords we use for online services, we can increase our security and better maintain our privacy.

This platform leverages the latest encryption techniques to deliver full end-to-end encryption. Moreover, users can apply extra security features (e.g. MFA, AWS keys) to establish their level of desired security. By providing a user-friendly interface and high availability, it aims to tidy up our online security measures.

Syste, Design Tenets

Security by design

It is impossible to lose, use, or abuse data one doesn’t possess. Therefore one designs systems to reduce the amount of sensitive user data stored or possible to be acquired.

Encryption works

When designing a system, one prefers security that is enforced by cryptography instead of enforced by software or personnel policy.

Comprehension & Transparency

One strives to fully understand all available and implemented tools. Commanding their strengths and weaknesses will maximize the ability to choose the correct tool for a particular task.

Audit & Testing

Through a suite of security tests, load tests and other tools, one can discover and eliminate vulnerabilities. Maintaining full test coverage ensures security during the development process.
